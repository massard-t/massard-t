About us

We make homes safe by creating easy and intelligent security products.

 

We believe home security should be easy, intelligent and affordable for everyone. From singles in the big city to families living in nature — our products are designed to make each and every home safe.

 

By combining powerful Artificial Intelligence with beautiful design, our products offer best of both worlds. We believe that technology is here to make life easier, so we develop by the following mantra: Keep it simple.
